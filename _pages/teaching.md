In addition to my research, I have undertaken various teaching roles during my time at the University of Oxford. In the 2021/2022 academic year I supervised a final year undergraduate Engineering Science student on their fourth year project, which focused on using reinforcement learning to stabilize an Inverted Pendulum. I was also appointed as a College Lecturer at Worcester College, teaching first and second year undergraduate Mathematics tutorials, which included: Statistics and Probability, Time-frequency Analysis, Partial Differential Equations, Ordinary Differential Equations, Linear Algebra and Vector Algebra. In addition I taught third year Control Theory tutorials and demonstrated their laboratory assessment.
